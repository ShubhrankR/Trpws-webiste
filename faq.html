FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1 What is The Rising People Welfare Society Organisation?

The Rising People Welfare Society is a Non-profit organisation that aims to build a better living ecosystem to the underprivileged children in New Delhi by providing them with the right education and care at the right time in life.

Q2 What is our Philosophy?

In today's world, everyone needs to be educated. To survive in this world, mere literacy isn't enough. Our kids are taught alphabets to alpha beta gama. We make sure that at the end of the day, the child doesn't only have fun but learn something of value.

Q3 Why should I care?

"Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world"
-Nelson Mandela
If you actively work/volunteer will help you to make a positive contribution in the growth and development of the society. You can work for the cause you are most passionate about. Everything you do to help an organization makes a difference. The support you provide has a ripple effect. It helps the organization, the individuals it serves, their respective families, their communities, and it helps you. It is impossible to make others feel good without feeling good yourself. Your support impacts the lives of many!

Q4 Can I volunteer?

Yes, you can volunteer at the NGO as an intern. There are following intern positions-Photography(and its editing),Social Worker, Content Writer, Web Development(with SEO knowledge).

Q5 What is the selection process?

  • Fill The Form on the Apply page first.
  • After a few days of its submission, you will receive a mail.
  • After that, you have visit and attend an induction session.

Q6 Will I get a certificate if I volunteer?

The skills that you gained can form a significant part of your CV. Your can impress your employer with your polished skills. This experience will also make your application stand out amongst your peers.

Q7 What are the benefits of joining the NGO?

Working for an NGO is better than sitting at home dreaming about an ideal society you want to live in. The only way to achieve an ideal society is, to make one, by working positively towards societal development.

Q8 Who is eligible to volunteer as an Intern?

Anyone who is above 18 years is free to join the NGO, however if you are below 18 years, you must have a consent of a parent or a guardian.

Q9 Can I contribute to the NGO by doing something working from home?

You definitely can, you can contribute us as work from home intern too.
